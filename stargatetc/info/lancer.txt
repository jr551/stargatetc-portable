Staff Weapon
==========================================================

Type: Goa'uld Plasma Weapon

The Staff Weapon is the primary fighing tool for the jaffa
The staff builds up plasma energy by passing pure hydrogen
back and forth at a rapid speed over a series of
superheated naquada shaft. When the firing trigger is
forces, the plasma is then ejected out of it’s
electromagnetic containment pod which is found on the
firing end of the staff. It also doubles as a melee weapon
for close range combat. The Goa'uld designed this weapon
to inflict fear and panic in their enemies. The staff has
been known to operate for centuries before requiring a
cell replacement.
