Zatarc Weapon
==========================================================

Type: Goa'uld Assassin Weapon
Action: Narrow path particle beam

The zatarc Weapon was originally designed for use by
Zatarcs (brain washed assassins) as a single strike sneak
attack weapon. The weapon has been adapted as a sniper
weapon for Jaffa on the battle field after The zatarcs
proved ineffective against their enemies.
