Hand Grenade
==========================================================

Type: Crude explosive weapon
Weight: 2.5 kg
Action: Squeeze handle & fuse

The Hand grenade is a simple explosive charge originally
designed for clearing trenches and foxholes. Its outer
shell is designed to create shrapnel and provide a easy
grip in the worst possible conditions. The hand grenade is
best used to clear paths before advancing or covering an
escape.
