Teal'c:
=======
Teal'c was once the First Prime of Apophis
One day he rebelled against his god and 
helped SG-1. From that day forward he has
been helping SG-1 and Earth in their battle
against the Goa'uld and he will not rest 
until all Jaffa are free. Amoung the
Goa'uld he is known as the Shol'va

=============

Speed: Medium

Armour: Light
