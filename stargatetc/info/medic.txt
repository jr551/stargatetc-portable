Epinephrine Pen
==========================================================

Type: Medical First Aid Tool
Action: Hyper dermic needle

A dose of Adrenalin mixed with modified Trutonin will
immediately heal a patient without developing any chemical
dependencies on the drug. This is primarily used as a
stopgap measure where medics are unavailable to sg teams.
The Adrenalin will also boost a patients stamina to full
giving the combatant the ability to defend him/her self.
