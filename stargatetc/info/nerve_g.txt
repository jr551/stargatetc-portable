Nerve Gas Grenade 
==========================================================

Type: Neural Depressant and Sedative
Action: Chemical release
 
Using the standard G62 gas canisters, the Nerve gas
grenade can be quickly released. Its primary effects are
impaired vision and sedation. Though its effects are
strong they quickly disappear after the exposure has
ended. Best tactical sue of this combat tool is ambush, to
be released at the foot of the enemy.
