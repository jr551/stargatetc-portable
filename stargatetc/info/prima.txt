First Prime (Serpent Guard):
======================
The First Prime is the head of the
personal guard of the System lord he
serves. He protects his lord at all
cost. The serpent guard wears a 
helmet which represents a serpent
it stands for the god he serves.
(A false god like Apophis)

Stats:
=============

Speed: Medium
Weapons: Heavy
Armour: Heavy

Special: Goa'uld Symbiote
